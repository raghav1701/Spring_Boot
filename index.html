  • 02 What are Resi Deck and Smart Deck?

    Resi Deck and Smart Deck represent the two main categories of Smart Wood. These are hardwoods which undergo a meticulous chemical cleaning process and are transformed from softwood to hardwood through a pressurization method, enriched with plant-derived ingredients. This process enhances their durability and longevity.
